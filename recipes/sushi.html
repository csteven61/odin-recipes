Smoked Salmon Sushi Roll

Salmon sushi rolls are surprisingly easy to make at home.

These sushi rolls are easy to make, and even easier on the wallet!

ingredients

  • 2 cups Japanese sushi rice
  • 6 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 6 sheets nori (dry seaweed)
  • 2 tablespoons wasabi paste
  • 8 ounces smoked salmon, cut into long strips
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and sliced
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and sliced

Steps

  1. Soak rice for 4 hours.
  2. Drain rice.
  3. Cook in a rice cooker with 2 cups of water. Rice must be slightly dry as vinegar will be added later.
  4. Immediately after rice is cooked, mix in rice wine vinegar
  5. Spread rice on a plate until completely cool.
  6. Place 1 sheet of seaweed on a bamboo mat.
  7. Press a thin layer of cool rice on seaweed, leaving at least a 1/2 inch top and bottom edge of the seaweed uncovered (for easier sealing later).
  8. Dot some wasabi on rice, and arrange: smoked salmon, cucumber, and avocado on rice, about 1 inch away from the bottom edge of seaweed.
  9. Slightly wet the top edge of seaweed, and roll up tightly from the bottom to the top edge with the help of the bamboo mat.
  10. Cut salmon roll into 8 equal pieces and serve.
