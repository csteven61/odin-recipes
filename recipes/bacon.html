Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeño Poppers

Crispy, creamy, and with the right amount of heat!

These poppers are definitely tastier than store-bought, and you can make them as spicy as you want!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup cream cheese
  • ½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 12 jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise, keep as many seeds as desired
  • 12 slices bacon

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. Mix cream cheese and Cheddar cheese together in a bowl until evenly blended.
  4. Fill each jalapeño half with cheese mixture.
  5. Put halves back together and wrap each stuffed pepper with a slice of bacon.
  6. Arrange bacon-wrapped peppers on the prepared baking sheet.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crispy, 25 to 35 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
